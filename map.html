Agriculture and Entrepreneurship Across India

India's agriculture is a mix of traditional crops and emerging agri-businesses. Farmers from all over the country are adopting modern techniques to grow crops such as rice, wheat, cotton, and spices. The country offers ample opportunities for aspiring agripreneurs in various sectors like organic farming, food processing, and agri-tech.
