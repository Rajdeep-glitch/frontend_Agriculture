About KrishiBharat

We are dedicated to transforming the agricultural industry through innovation, technology, and entrepreneurship.

Our Core Values

  • Innovation: Leveraging modern technology for smarter farming.
  • Sustainability: Promoting eco-friendly agricultural practices.
  • Community Engagement: Connecting farmers, agripreneurs, and experts.
  • Education: Providing training and workshops on agribusiness.

Empowering Agripreneurs

We focus on empowering aspiring agripreneurs by providing:

  • Access to funding and investment opportunities.
  • Training on business models, marketing, and farm management.
  • Guidance on government policies and subsidies.
  • Networking opportunities with successful agricultural entrepreneurs.

Technology in Agriculture

Technology is revolutionizing the agricultural sector. At KrishiBharat, we support farmers in adopting:

  • Precision Farming: Using sensors and AI for optimized crop production.
  • Drone Technology: Aerial monitoring for better farm management.
  • Hydroponics & Vertical Farming: Innovative methods to increase yield.
  • Blockchain in Agriculture: Ensuring transparency in the supply chain.

Our Impact

Since our inception, KrishiBharat has helped:

  • Over 10,000 farmers transition to sustainable farming.
  • More than 500 agripreneurs launch their startups.
  • Conduct 100+ workshops on modern agricultural practices.
  • Establish direct market connections for farmers to sell their produce.
